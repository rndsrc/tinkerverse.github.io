You may sometimes choose to not provide certain information. However, this may limit the way we can provide you with our Services. In general, we collect information to provide you with our Services, to develop our products and services, to operate our business, to better engage with you, and to improve our Services. Like other online services, we use “cookies” and other unique identifiers when you use our Services. These cookies and identifies help our systems to recognize your browser or device and to provide and improve our Services. For more information on cookies, please visit the FTC’s Publication on internet cookies here: https://www.ftc.gov/policy-notices/privacy-policy/internet-cookies. With your permission, we may also collect and use the voice and visual data from your camera and microphone, whether temporarily or for a longer period, for example to provide, troubleshoot, and improve our Services related to Augmented Reality and related voice-controlled services.

We use your personal information to provide you with our Services, to develop our products and services, to operate our business, to better engage with you, including to share promotional offers with you, and to improve our Services, including to troubleshoot and fix errors, to analyze performances, to provide functionality, to improve user experience, recommend features, products and services that may be of interest to you, to tailor to your preferences, and to personalize your experience with our Services. We also use your information to comply with legal obligations, including to comply with local laws and regulations, and to secure and safeguard our users and Services. We may also use your information to communicate with you through our Services, or by other means, for example, in connection with outstanding payments. We may also use your personal information to display and tailor interest-based advertisements that may be of interest to you. We may also use personal information to safeguard our users and Services, to prevent and detect fraud or abuse, and to enforce our Terms of Use.

It is possible that we do not collect or use all the types of information described here. We are not in the business of selling our user’s personal information to others, but information about our users is important for us to provide and improve our Services, and is important to our business. We may share your personal information with our affiliates, including third party service providers including, Apple Apps Store, Google AdSense, Amazon Cloud Services, and Microsoft Azure, to provide, improve, and personalize our Services to you. We may also share your personal information with Third Parties, for example, to process your payment. In the event of a business transfer, we may also share our user information with relevant parties and agents connected to the transaction, including lawyers, financial advisors, and government regulators during due diligence. When required, we may release account and other personal information to comply with the law, to enforce or apply our Terms of Use and other agreements, or protect the rights, property, or safety of our users, our Services, our business, or others. This includes exchanging information with other companies, organizations, and government agencies for fraud protection and credit risk reduction. In certain cases, you may have an opportunity to opt-out from sharing your personal information.

Data Security

We work to adhere to the best practices to safeguard data. We work to protect your personal information during data transmission by using encryption protocols and software. We work to follow the Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard (PCI DSS) during handling of credit card data. We work to maintain physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards in connection with the collection, storage, and sharing of user personal information. For example, we may ask to verify your identity before we share your personal information to you. It is important for you to protect against unauthorized access to your computers, devices, and accounts. Your password for our Services should be unique from other online or offline services. We recommend signing off from your account when you use our Services from a shared computer.

Advertisement

Our Services may include third-party advertising and links to other online services. Our third-party advertising affiliates may collect information about you when you interact on their services. We may provide advertisement companies with information for a more tailored ad experiences. We do not share your name or other information that directly identifies you when we do this. Instead, we use an encoded advertisement identifier like a cookie, a device identifier, or a code. This helps us to, for example, not serve an ad to download our apps after you have already done so.
